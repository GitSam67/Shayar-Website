What is Mausam?

Mausam is an emerging platform to connect with the people sharing poetic verse altogether. It is a competition ensuring an opportunity given to poets to build a community with common interests. It helps to make overall growth of your personality by expressing your thoughts via words and sentences.

How to participate in this competition?

To participate in this contest, we just need to register ourselves from the website. But, firstly you need to sign-up with your e-mail address.

Any cost to participate?

No, the Shayar community is providing FREE participation in Mausam Event. You just need to register yourself for the same with your basic details.

What is meant by featuring in Magazine?

Shayar community is there with its company’s magazine to represent their organization. This magazine will include the best poetries by top contestants who will compete with several other competitors to represent themselves. It’s a digital magazine such as considering e-books, pdfs, etc.

Is there any language barrier?

No, there is no language barrier. You can just upload your poetry in your preferred language.

How can be this website helpful?

This website will help us to know about the competition and its basic features to communicate. Various poets are here to build a community and find a way to connect with poets all around them.
